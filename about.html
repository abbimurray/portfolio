About

Career

Hi, I’m Abbi — a passionate and curious software development student at SETU Carlow. I love building projects that combine creativity with logic, and I’m especially driven by a desire to make technology more accessible and impactful.

My journey into tech began with a fascination for how things work. I started coding as a hobby, and it quickly turned into a passion. I enjoy the challenge of problem-solving and the satisfaction of creating something from scratch.

Currently, I’m undertaking a six-month internship as part of my third year, working as a Business Applications Intern at Kildare County Council. This hands-on role is helping me further develop my technical skills while gaining real-world experience in public sector digital transformation.

My Education

I’m currently in my third year of the Software Development degree at SETU Carlow, where I’ve consistently aimed to excel both academically and practically.
My coursework has included topics like Advanced Data Structures and Algorithms (94%), Cloud Development (88%), and Web Programming and Databases (98%).
In Year Two, I gained experience with full-stack development, systems design, and team collaboration — notably completing a car rental management system project with a score of 93%.
In Year One, I built a strong foundation in Java programming, networking, operating systems, and computer hardware.
